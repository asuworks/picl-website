Getting your Gemini API key

picl uses Google's Gemini AI to generate listings. You'll need a free API key to get started.

  1. Go to Google AI Studio
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Click Create API key
  4. Select any Google Cloud project (or create a new one)
  5. Copy the generated key
  6. Open picl, go to Settings, and paste your key

The free tier is generous and sufficient for personal use. Your key is stored encrypted on your device using Android's secure keystore and is only sent directly to Google's API ‚Äî never to us or any third party.

You can check your usage and quota anytime at AI Studio.

Google Drive export

When you export listings, picl uploads images to Google Drive and appends listing data to a Google Sheets spreadsheet ‚Äî all within your own Google account.

Minimal permissions

picl requests only the drive.file scope. This means it can only access files it created ‚Äî it cannot read, modify, or even see any other files in your Drive. This is the most restrictive Drive scope available.

What gets created

  • A picl folder in your Drive containing uploaded item photos
  • A picl Listings spreadsheet with columns: title, description, price, condition, category, city, neighborhood, zip, and image links

No data collection

picl has no backend servers. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data ‚Äî not your photos, not your listings, not your Google account information. All communication happens directly between your device and Google's APIs over encrypted HTTPS connections. Your Google credentials are managed entirely by Google's sign-in SDK and are never stored or transmitted by picl.

You can revoke access at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
